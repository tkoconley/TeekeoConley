Where I'm From

I was born and raised in Mississippi; my family and I moved a lot, but most of my teen years took place in a small town called Grenada, a place I frequently describe like the tiny island town of Swallow Falls from Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs.
I wanted to venture out, beyond the South, and that led me to the University of Missouri-Columbia to study journalism. Pretty soon now, I'll be graduating with a bachelor's in Journalism with an emphasis in strategic communication. M-I-Z-Z-O-U!

What I Do

My time at the University of Missouri was an enlightening, exhilirating rollercoaster; I discovered a passion for creating and telling stories for brands. This love for strategy has unfortunately prohibited me from ever looking at an advertisement without thinking, "Hm. That was a pretty good hook," or, "How did this tagline make it through the draft phase?" On the bright side, it's enjoyable to think analytical about these things because of my experience in research, branding, campaign development, and marketing strategy.

That's a bit about me, but I'd love to hear from you! Thank you for visiting my website!

I'm always open to connecting, collaborating, or discussing new opportunities!
