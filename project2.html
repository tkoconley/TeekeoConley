My Time in AdZou as a Copywriter

During my time as a Copywriter at AdZou, I had the opportunity to work within a fast-paced, collaborative environment where strategy and creativity came together to solve real-world brand challenges. AdZou is the University of Missouri's student-run advertising agency, and also my capstone, and I was proud to be part of a eight-person team working on behalf of the Novak Leadership Foundation.

Over the course of 16 weeks, our team was tasked with developing a comprehensive brand strategy that would authentically connect with the foundation's target audience while staying true to its mission of inspiring leadership and community impact. My role as copywriter placed me at the intersection of research and storytelling; I was shaping how the brand spoke and who it spoke to.

To get there, we, my team, conducted both primary and secondary research, including surveys, competitive market analysis, and in-depth interviews designed to uncover the motivations, values, and behaviors of the foundation's audience, which was administrators and educators. That research directly informed the brand messaging I developed, ensuring every word had purpose and resonated with real people. I worked closely with my teammates to translate those insights into strategic tactics and branding solutions that were clear, compelling, and aligned with the client's goals.

This experience deepened my understanding of what it means to write with intention and to work with a good-sized team. AdZou gave me a space to grow as both a writer and a strategic thinker, and working on a project with real community impact made every deadline feel worthwhile.

I'm always open to connecting, collaborating, or discussing new opportunities!
